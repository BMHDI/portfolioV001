AI Article Summarizer

Imagine cutting through long articles in seconds—just paste a link, and the AI Article Summarizer instantly delivers a crisp, clear summary of the key points. This tool is perfect for those who want to stay informed without getting bogged down by lengthy reads. With a built-in history feature, users can revisit all their past summaries, creating a personal archive of insights from across the web. Powered by GPT-4, the AI intelligently extracts the most important information, ensuring each summary is accurate and meaningful. It's not just a tool—it's a smarter way to digest content.

Allo Alberta

Allo Alberta is a mobile app dedicated to the Francophone community in Alberta, offering essential resources and information for both long-time residents and newcomers. The app features local news updates in French, valuable tools for newcomers, and an intuitive interface for easy navigation. Developed using Adalo, this was my first coding project, igniting my passion for software development and prompting my career shift. Allo Alberta enhances community connection among Francophones in Alberta, providing a vital platform for information and resources.
